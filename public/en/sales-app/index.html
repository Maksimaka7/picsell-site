Distributor Pain Points

Field Sales is Chaos Without a System

Three problems that cost your business every day — and the solution that solves each of them.

⏰

"Agents spend 30% of their day on Excel and WhatsApp instead of selling"

Manual order entry, collecting reports, phone coordination — 2-3 hours of routine per agent per day. Multiply that by your team.

PICSELL solves this: Smart Orders automatically generate orders based on stock levels and dynamics — the agent just confirms.
🗺️

"Routes are planned from memory — 30% of outlets are missed or duplicated"

A suboptimal route = extra hours in traffic + missed clients + reduced coverage. Real money burning every day.

PICSELL solves this: Route Manager optimizes the route in 1 click — drag-and-drop on the map, saving 40% travel time.
🔎

"The supervisor doesn't know where the agent is or what they actually did until end of day"

GPS is off, reports come in the evening, verification is impossible. Fake check-ins. Missed outlets. You pay for work nobody controls.

PICSELL solves this: Supervisor View — real team map, GPS verification of every visit, KPIs in real time.
