Vision AI Features

Vision AI Sees More Than Just Products

Every photo is a structured report. Six metrics automatically — with no human involvement.

⛔

OOS Detection

The system automatically detects empty shelf positions and generates an alert. From photo to manager notification — 2 minutes. Not next month, not next week.

⚡ Alert in 2 minutes
📊

Share of Shelf

Exact shelf share of your brand in % by each SKU and competitor. Dynamic comparison — weekly, monthly, quarterly. Across every chain and region.

Per outlet
💰

Price Monitoring

AI recognizes shelf price tags and compares them against recommended prices. Dumping is detected automatically. Price report across all chains — in real time.

Dumping = auto-alert
🏆

Perfect Store Score

Automatic scoring of compliance with shelf standards for each outlet. A single metric understood by everyone — from the field agent to the CEO.

Henkel: 87% Score
🛡️

Anti-Fraud System

GPS tags, photo metadata analysis, gallery upload prohibition, shooting angle verification. Fake reports are detected automatically — the agent won't even notice.

0% Fakes
🎯

POSM Control

Presence and correct placement of POS materials — wobblers, shelf talkers, price strips. You know which promotional materials are in place, and which are missing or incorrectly installed.

Promo under control
