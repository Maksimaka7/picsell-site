Where Brands Lose Money

Four Pain Points of Every Brand Manager

Every manufacturer faces these problems. The question is: how much will ignoring them continue to cost?

⛔

OOS without alerts: competitors take your shelf space

You find out about Out-of-Stock from a monthly report — when a competitor has already taken your spot. Every empty shelf costs you customers you'll never get back.

PICSELL solves this: OOS Detection — manager alert within 2 minutes after the agent's photo.
🎭

Agent reports: fake photos, manipulation — you pay for nothing

An agent sent a photo of the "correct" shelf — but it was taken a week ago or in the wrong outlet. GPS can be bypassed. Excel reports can be faked. Without a system — you can't prove it.

PICSELL solves this: Anti-Fraud — GPS tags, photo metadata, shooting angle analysis — automatically.
💸

Promo without control: you paid for placement — verification is impossible

The retail chain confirmed placement of shelf talkers and wobblers. But nobody checks if they're actually there. You pay for promo — and don't know if it's working.

PICSELL solves this: POSM Control — promotional material presence recorded automatically at every visit.
📊

Perfect Store only in PowerPoint — the reality on shelves is different

Shelf standards are approved, the planogram is beautiful. But who checks whether it's followed in each of the 31,000 outlets? Nobody. That's why the real picture and the presentation are different things.

PICSELL solves this: Perfect Store Score — automatic scoring of every outlet across all KPIs.
