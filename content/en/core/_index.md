PICSELL Core is the foundation of the field-execution platform. Every role runs on it: merchandisers, sales reps, supervisors and managers.

## Core capabilities

- **Routes, visits, GPS** — route planning and GPS visit control.
- **Tasks + photo reports + tagging** — tasks with photo reports and tagging of products and issues.
- **Store Check + Perfect Store** — outlet audit and Perfect Store score per visit.
- **Assortment matrices + price monitoring** — assortment and shelf-price control.
- **Merch planning + work-time control** — merch schedules and work-time tracking.

Core is the base that Vision, Field, Radar and HoReCa run on.
